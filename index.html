About Me

I'm a passionate software developer and entrepreneur with over 5 years of experience building scalable applications that improve lives. Based in Nairobi, Kenya, I specialize in full-stack web & mobile development, creating solutions across various industries including real estate, fitness, automotive, healthcare, and social innovation.

My journey in tech began when I discovered the power of code to transform ideas into reality. Since then, I've dedicated myself to continuous learning and growth, working on numerous projects that have impacted thousands of users across Kenya and beyond.
